About the Bakery

Started at a home oven in Shahpura, moved when the orders outgrew the kitchen.

Sweet Crumbs began in 2021 taking WhatsApp orders for birthday cakes in a Shahpura flat. Three years and a lot of 5am starts later, it's a proper storefront in MP Nagar with a small team of four bakers — still no artificial cream, no shortcut fondant, and still closed the day after Diwali because everyone deserves one day off.
