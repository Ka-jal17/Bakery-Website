About

Our Story

Baked from Scratch,
Made with Love

Sweet Crumbs was founded on a simple belief: that baking should never be rushed, and shortcuts should never be taken. Every layer, every frosting, every cookie that leaves our kitchen in MP Nagar is handmade using the finest ingredients.

We started as a home-based passion project and grew into a beloved neighborhood bakery because our customers knew they could taste the care in every bite. No mixes. No preservatives. No shortcuts.

Today, we bake fresh every morning and deliver the same day—still warm on pastries, perfectly set on cakes. That's the Sweet Crumbs promise.

🌾 Farm to Counter

We source local ingredients whenever possible, supporting our community and ensuring freshness in every bite.

🍫 No Shortcuts

No cake mixes, no frozen components, no preservatives. Everything is made from scratch, in small batches, daily.

⏰ Same-Day Magic

Order by noon, receive by evening. Your cake is never more than a few hours old. Taste the difference freshness makes.
